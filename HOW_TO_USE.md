# How to use Karne 🥩

Your orders, stock, money, customers, and full history — all in one place.
Open it on a phone, tablet, or computer: **https://vgrateja-jpg.github.io/karne/**

## Signing in
Open the link, type your **email** and **password**, tap **Sign in**.
Tip: on a phone, use the browser menu → *Add to Home screen* so it opens like an app.

## Finding your way around
There's a menu on the left (tap **☰** on a phone), grouped so things are easy to find:
- **Daily** — Home, New order, Text orders, Orders, Deliveries
- **Stock** — Butchering, Prices, Stock
- **People** — Customers, Suppliers
- **Money** — Cash & Banks, Cash count, Receivables, Expenses, Cheques, Loans
- **Reports & setup** — Daily Report, Monthly Report, Historical, Settings

---

## Taking orders
**New order** → pick the customer (or *Cash / walk-in*), choose products (the **sale price** fills
in automatically), type the quantity, **Save**. Stock and the customer's balance update by themselves.
- If the customer is behind on payments, a warning shows (and their credit limit, if you set one).
- **Text orders** — orders texted to your number show up here to review and **✓ Confirm** (once
  the SMS setup is done; see `SMS_SETUP.md`).
- **Orders** — the list of all orders. Tap one to open it.

**Order page** — open any order to:
- **adjust the actual delivered weight** / fix prices, add or remove items, **Save changes**
- **Mark delivered → Mark paid**
- **Void** (cancels it, returns stock, keeps the record) or **Delete** (removes it entirely)
- **Print receipt**

**Deliveries** — pick a date to see everything to send out that day, **Mark delivered**, and
**🖨 Print** the day's delivery list for the driver.

## Stock
- **Butchering** — when a whole animal comes in (e.g. 200 kg cow), record it, list each cut with its
  weight; cuts are added to **Stock**, and a new cut name is added to **Prices** to set its price.
  Shows the yield. (Deleting a butchering removes its cuts from stock and the price list.)
- **Prices** — your price list. Each product has a **Unit price** (base) and a **Sale price** (what
  customers pay). Pick any unit (kg, g, lb, pc, box…). Change a price once — used everywhere after.
- **Stock** — how much of each product you have, its value, and **Record stock in** for deliveries.

---

## People
- **Customers** — everyone you sell to, with the money they owe. Set an optional **credit limit**.
  Tap a name to open their **ledger**:
  - full statement with a **running balance**, **printable** / Save as PDF
  - **Record a payment** — type a partial amount or tap **Pay full**; with date + method
  - **Add a charge** straight onto the ledger
  - **search**, **sort**, and **delete** a wrong entry
- **Suppliers** — who you buy from: record purchases (incl. cattle by weight × price), see the
  **balance you owe** each supplier, and record your payments to them.

---

## Money
- **Cash & Banks** — every account's balance; add/edit/delete accounts; record deposits,
  withdrawals, and transfers.
- **Cash count** — end of day: it shows the **expected cash** (cash sales + collected − cash
  expenses) and you enter what you **counted**; it shows the difference.
- **Receivables** — who owes you, aged **0–30 / 31–60 / 61–90 / 90+ days** so you know who to chase.
- **Expenses** — log salaries, diesel, utilities, etc.
- **Cheques** — record cheques in/out with due dates and status (pending → cleared).
- **Loans** — money you borrowed or lent, with running balances.

---

## Reports
- **Daily Report** — today at a glance; a fresh day starts blank. **Download PDF**.
- **Monthly Report** — the month by day (actual dates, incl. closed days), by product, by customer.
  Tap a day for its detail. **Download PDF**.
- **Historical** — your full history: tap a **year → quarters → months**, tap a month for its report.
- **Home** — today's sales, money owed to you, money you owe, cash on hand, and this month's totals.

**To save a report:** tap **Download PDF** and choose *Save as PDF* — store it on your computer/drive.

## Settings
Your shop name/address (for receipts), the phone numbers that text orders, and your branches.

---

## Good to know
- It saves to the cloud automatically — nothing to "save to a file."
- Works on any device with internet; same login everywhere.
- Nothing is locked — you can edit a price, a customer, an order, or record a correction anytime.

Questions or something you'd like changed? Just ask. 🙂
