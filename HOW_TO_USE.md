# How to use Karne 🥩

Your orders, stock, money, and customer balances — all in one place.
Open it on your phone, tablet, or computer: **https://vgrateja-jpg.github.io/karne/**

---

## Signing in
Open the link, type your **email** and **password**, tap **Sign in**.
Tip: on a phone, use the browser menu → *Add to Home screen* so it opens like an app.

## Finding your way around
There's a menu on the left (tap **☰** on a phone). It's grouped so things are easy to find:
- **Daily** — Home, New order, Text orders, Orders
- **People** — Customers, Suppliers
- **Money** — Cash & banks, Expenses, Cheques, Loans
- **Stock** — Stock, Prices
- **Reports & setup** — Monthly report, Settings

---

## The daily flow

### ✍️ Take an order
1. Tap **New order**.
2. Pick the **customer** (or leave it as *Cash / walk-in*).
3. Add each item: choose the **product** (the price fills in automatically), type the **quantity**.
   Tap **+ Add line** for more items.
4. Tap **Save order**. ✅ That's it — the stock and the customer's balance update by themselves.
5. Tap **Print receipt** if you need a copy.

### 📩 Orders that come by text
When a customer texts an order, it shows up under **Text orders**.
- Check the customer and the items (fix anything that looks off).
- Tap **✓ Confirm order** to turn it into a real order, or **Ignore** if it's not an order.

---

## People & money owed
- **Customers** = everyone you sell to, with the **money they owe you** beside each name.
  Tap a name for their **statement**, and **Record a payment** when they pay.
- **Suppliers** = who you buy from, with the **money you owe them**; record purchases and your
  payments to them here too.

## Money
- **Cash & banks** — every account's balance; record deposits, withdrawals, and transfers.
- **Expenses** — log salaries, diesel, utilities, etc.
- **Cheques** — list cheques in/out with due dates; mark them deposited/cleared.
- **Loans** — money you borrowed or lent, with running balances.

## Stock
- **Stock** shows how much of each product you have (it goes down automatically as you sell).
  Use **Record stock in** when new stock arrives.
- **Butchering** — when a whole animal comes in (e.g. a 200 kg cow), record it, then list the
  cuts and their weights. Each cut is added to your stock, and it shows the yield (how much you
  got vs the whole-animal weight).
- **Prices** is your price list — change a price once and it's used everywhere from then on
  (past receipts keep the price they were sold at).

## Reports & setup
- **Monthly report** — the whole month at a glance: each day's sales, by product, by customer.
  Use **‹ Prev / Next ›** to change month and **🖨 Print** for paper.
- **Home** — today's sales, money owed to you, money you owe, and cash on hand.
- **Settings** — your shop name/address (for receipts), text-order senders, and branches.

---

## Good to know
- It saves to the cloud automatically — nothing to "save to a file."
- Works on any device with internet; sign in with the same account everywhere.
- If a number ever looks wrong, you can edit it — nothing is locked.

Questions or something you'd like changed? Just ask. 🙂
