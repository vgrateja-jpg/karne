# How to use Karne 🥩

Your orders, inventory, and customer balances — all in one place.
Open it on your phone, tablet, or computer: **https://vgrateja-jpg.github.io/karne/**

---

## Signing in
Open the link, type your **email** and **password**, tap **Sign in**.
Tip: on a phone, use the browser menu → *Add to Home screen* so it opens like an app.

---

## The daily flow

### ✍️ Take an order
1. Tap **New Order**.
2. Pick the **customer** (or leave it as *Cash / walk-in*).
3. Add each item: choose the **product** (the price fills in automatically), type the **quantity**.
   Tap **+ Add line** for more items.
4. Tap **Save order**. ✅ That's it — the stock and the customer's balance update by themselves.
5. Tap **Print receipt** if you need a copy.

### 📩 Orders that come by text (SMS Inbox)
When a customer texts an order, it shows up under **Inbox**.
- Check the customer and the items (fix anything that looks off).
- Tap **✓ Confirm order** to turn it into a real order, or **Ignore** if it's not an order.

---

## Customers & money owed
- **Customers** tab = everyone you sell to, with the **balance they owe** beside each name.
- Tap a name to see their **statement** (every order and payment, with a running balance).
- When they pay, open their statement → **Record a payment** (amount, date, cash/GCash/bank).

---

## Stock
- **Inventory** shows how much of each product you have on hand (it goes down automatically as you sell).
- When new stock comes in, use **Record stock in** (product, quantity, date).

---

## Seeing your records
- **Monthly** = the whole month at a glance: each day's sales, totals by product, and totals by
  customer. Use **‹ Prev / Next ›** to change month, and **🖨 Print** for a paper copy.
- **Dashboard** = today's sales and the total everyone owes you.

---

## Prices & shop details
- **Products** = your price list. Change a price once here and it's used everywhere from then on.
  (Past receipts keep the price they were sold at.)
- **Settings** = your shop name, address, and phone that appear on printed receipts.

---

## Good to know
- It saves to the cloud automatically — nothing to "save to a file."
- Works on any device with internet; sign in with the same account everywhere.
- If a number ever looks wrong, you can edit the customer, the price, or record a correction —
  nothing is locked.

Questions or something you'd like changed? Just ask. 🙂
